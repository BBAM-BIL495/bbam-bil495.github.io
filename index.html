Project Overview

Body & Beyond AI Mentor is an AI-powered mobile application designed to act as a fitness coach, where users can create custom workout plans to manage their health. By utilizing real-time pose estimation via the device's camera, the app provides instant audio and visual feedbacks to ensure users perform exercises with correct form, reducing injury risks and enhancing training efficiency.

Personalized Plans

Create custom workout plans, set reminders, and track your progress through a detailed dashboard.

Real-Time Analysis

Receive audio and visual feedbacks to correct your posture and improve exercise efficiency in real-time.

Privacy First

Your personal data is protected, raw camera footage is never stored or shared.
